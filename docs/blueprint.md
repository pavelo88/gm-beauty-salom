# **App Name**: GM Beauty House

## Core Features:

- Dynamic Content Management: Ability for an administrator to add, modify, and delete services, products, projects, and menu items, which are then dynamically displayed on the client-facing site.
- AI Stylist Assistant Tool: A generative AI tool that provides personalized beauty and barbering style recommendations based on client events or preferences, acting as a virtual stylist for GM Beauty House's Salon & Barber Shop.
- AI Personal Shopper Tool: A generative AI tool for the 'Boutique Editorial' section that suggests outfits or perfumes tailored to a user's occasion and preferences, offering curated fashion advice.
- AI Entertainment Sommelier Tool: A generative AI tool in the 'Lounge & Resto' section that suggests combinations of food, drinks, and gaming options to enhance the client's waiting experience.
- Multi-Section Client Interface: A responsive user interface segmented into 'Home', 'Salón & Barbería' (Beauty), 'Boutique Editorial', 'Modulares GM' (Alliance), and 'Lounge & Resto' sections, each with its distinct visual style and dynamic content display.
- Image & Content Galleries: Carousel or grid displays for projects, products, and services, showcasing visual content relevant to each section of the 'GM Beauty House'.
- Firebase Backend Integration: Utilizes Firebase Authentication for admin access and Firestore for storing and serving all dynamic content, ensuring real-time data synchronization.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A luxurious and vibrant gold (#FFDB01), representing elegance and premium quality. It serves as a striking highlight against dark backgrounds, derived from the app's established 'GOLD_VIBRANT' palette for its high-end brand.
- Background color: A very dark, almost black hue with a subtle warm undertone (#11100E), maintaining focus on content and accentuating the primary gold, offering a sleek, premium, and sophisticated base.
- Accent color: A sophisticated rose gold (#D4A373), providing a soft, warm contrast to the primary gold and dark background, especially in specific sections or for subtle embellishments. This hue is drawn from the existing 'ROSE_GOLD' theme of the app for a cohesive palette.
- Headline font: 'Alegreya' (serif), chosen for its elegant, intellectual, and contemporary feel, lending a high-end, sophisticated touch to titles and prominent text.
- Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif), providing a modern, clear, and objective readability that complements the serif headlines without distracting from them.
- Consistent use of crisp, modern line icons (from Lucide React) to maintain a sleek and sophisticated aesthetic across all sections and functions of the application.
- Adopt a clean, spacious, and responsive layout with a clear hierarchy of information. Utilize a hero section for impact, grid systems for presenting content like services and products, and tabbed navigation for logical content segmentation.
- Subtle and smooth animations, such as `fade-in`, `slide-in`, and graceful hover effects, to enhance user interaction and provide a fluid, premium browsing experience without being intrusive.
